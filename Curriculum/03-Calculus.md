# Calculus

## Relevance to Quantum Physics

The Schrödinger equation is a differential equation. Expectation values require integration. Wavefunctions are normalised via integrals. Calculus is the tool used to extract physical predictions from quantum theory.

2. **Differentiation** — rules, chain rule, physical meaning as rate of change
3. **Integration** — definite/indefinite, techniques, area and accumulation
4. **Differential Equations** — ODEs, separation of variables → Schrödinger equation
5. **Multivariable Calculus** — partial derivatives, gradient, divergence
6. **Complex Calculus Basics** — differentiation of complex functions
