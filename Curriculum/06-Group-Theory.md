# Group Theory

## Relevance to Quantum Physics

Symmetry is a central organising principle of physics. Group theory is the mathematics of symmetry. Conservation laws arise from symmetry groups (Noether's theorem). The spin of particles, the structure of the periodic table, and quantum field theory all rely on group representations (SU(2), SU(3), U(1)).

4. **Representations** — mapping groups to matrices → how symmetry acts on quantum states
