# Complex Numbers

## Relevance to Quantum Physics

Quantum states are described by complex-valued wavefunctions. Probability amplitudes are complex numbers, and their modulus squared gives probabilities. Euler's formula $e^{i\theta} = \cos\theta + i\sin\theta$ underpins the time evolution of quantum states.
