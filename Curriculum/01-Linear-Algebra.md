# Linear Algebra

## Relevance to Quantum Physics

Linear algebra is the language of quantum mechanics. Quantum states are vectors in a Hilbert space, observables are linear operators, and measurement outcomes are eigenvalues. Understanding this topic deeply is the foundation for everything that follows.

## Subtopics

1. **Vectors** — geometric intuition, operations, basis
2. **Vector Spaces** — abstract definition, span, linear independence
3. **Linear Transformations** — maps between spaces, matrix representation
4. **Matrices** — operations, inverses, special forms
5. **Determinants** — geometric meaning, computation
6. **Eigenvalues & Eigenvectors** — definition, computation, diagonalisation → quantum states & observables
7. **Inner Product Spaces** — dot products generalised, orthonormality, projections → probability amplitudes
8. **Tensor Products** — combining spaces → composite quantum systems, entanglement
