# Exercise 8: Eigenvalues and Geometric Interpretation

## Problem

Consider the matrix:

$$B = \begin{pmatrix} 2 & 0 \\ 0 & 5 \end{pmatrix}$$

1. Without computing, **predict** the eigenvalues and eigenvectors geometrically. (Hint: what kind of transformation is this?)
2. Verify your prediction using the characteristic equation.
3. What is $\det(B)$, and how does it relate to the eigenvalues? Can you spot a pattern?
