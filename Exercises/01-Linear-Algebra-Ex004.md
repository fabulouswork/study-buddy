# Exercise 4: Matrix Multiplication (Composition)

## Problem

You have two transformations represented as matrices:

$$A = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 2 \\ 0 & 1 \end{pmatrix}$$

$$B = \begin{pmatrix} 2 & 0 \\ 1 & 3 \end{pmatrix}$$

**Compute $B \cdot A$** (apply $A$ first, then $B$).

## Interpretation

Once you've computed the product, describe in words: what does each column of $B \cdot A$ represent?

---

**Hint (if needed):** Remember, the entry in row $i$, column $j$ of $B \cdot A$ is the dot product of row $i$ of $B$ with column $j$ of $A$.
