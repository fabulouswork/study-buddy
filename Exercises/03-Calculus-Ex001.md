# Exercise: u-Substitution (Ex001)

Evaluate the indefinite integral:

\[
\int \frac{2x}{x^2 + 5} \, dx
\]

## Your task

1. Choose a substitution \(u = \ ?\)
2. Rewrite \(dx\)-part in terms of \(du\)
3. Convert the whole integral into \(u\)
4. Integrate
5. Substitute back to \(x\)

Write your full working.
