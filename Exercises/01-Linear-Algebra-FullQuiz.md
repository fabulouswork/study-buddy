# Full Topic Quiz — Linear Algebra

Covering all 8 subtopics. Answer each question with your reasoning.

---

**Q1 — Vectors**
Find a unit vector in the direction of **v** = (3, -4). What is its length?

---

**Q2 — Vector Spaces**
Is the set of all 2×2 matrices with zero trace a vector space under standard matrix addition and scalar multiplication? Justify briefly.

---

**Q3 — Linear Transformations**
Let T: ℝ³ → ℝ² be defined by T(x, y, z) = (x - y, 2x + z). Find the matrix representation of T with respect to the standard bases of ℝ³ and ℝ².

---

**Q4 — Matrices**
Let A = [[1, 2], [3, 4]]. Compute A². Then compute A⁻¹ (if it exists).

---

**Q5 — Determinants**
Compute the determinant of B = [[2, 0, 1], [1, 1, 0], [0, 2, 3]]. What does the determinant tell you about the invertibility of B?

---

**Q6 — Eigenvalues & Eigenvectors**
Find the eigenvalues and eigenvectors of A = [[2, 1], [1, 2]].

---

**Q7 — Inner Product Spaces**
Compute the inner product of **u** = (1, 0, i) and **v** = (i, 2, 1) in ℂ³ with the standard Hermitian inner product. Are they orthogonal? Normalise **u**.

---

**Q8 — Tensor Products**
Let **u** = (1, 2) and **v** = (3, 4) in ℝ². Compute **u** ⊗ **v** as a 4-component vector.

Then: determine whether |ψ⟩ = (1, 0, 0, 1) is separable or entangled. Explain your reasoning as we did in the lesson.
